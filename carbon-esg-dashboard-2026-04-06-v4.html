McKinsey ESG Scorecard Methodology

Scoring dimensions, sub-dimensions, and aggregation rules for the scorecard built from the 2023 McKinsey engagement. This is the first application.

DimensionDefinitionSub-scoresFinancial Translation
E1 Physical ClimateFacility exposure to floods, hurricanes, extreme heat, water stressFlood · Heat · Hurricane · WaterInsurance premium trajectory · revenue-days at risk · capex for hardening
E2 Transition RiskCarbon intensity, regulatory exposure, customer decarb pressure, stranded assetsCarbon intensity · Regulatory · Customer pressure · Stranding$/yr carbon cost · exit multiple risk · revenue at risk from customer churn
E3 Environmental LiabilityChemicals, waste, compliance history, contaminationChemicals · Waste · Compliance · ContaminationPollution liability premium · remediation reserve · enforcement exposure
S1 WorkforceSafety, turnover, trainingSafety · Turnover · TrainingWorkers comp · OSHA · turnover cost
S2 CommunityCommunity impact, diversityImpact · DiversityReputational risk premium
G1 GovernanceBoard oversight, disclosure readiness, ethicsBoard · Disclosure · EthicsLP disclosure cost · litigation risk
  • Scoring scale: 1 (low risk) · 2 (limited) · 3 (moderate) · 4 (elevated) · 5 (high/material)
  • Sub-scores: Each dimension is decomposed into 2-4 sub-components, each scored 1-5. Dimension score = max of sub-scores (a material sub-factor cannot be diluted).
  • Composite: max(E1, E2, E3) — environmental risks dominate; a 5 on any E dimension flags the portco as high-risk regardless of S/G performance.
  • Financial framing mandatory: every dimension carries a $ estimate. No standalone environmental metrics. Per protocol.
  • Trajectory: improving (↘), stable (→), or deteriorating (↗). Forward-looking based on regulatory pipeline, customer contracts, and portco-specific factors.
  • Refresh cadence: quarterly update for S1/G1 (reflect operational reality), annual refresh for E dimensions (post-carbon inventory).

QA Protocol

  1. Source Verification. Every facility emission traces to CSV row + EPA factor.
  2. Structural Consistency. Facility → portco → portfolio totals reconcile (24,613 mt CO₂e). Intensity metrics match the financial denominators pulled from Supabase.
  3. Adversarial Review. 50 facility rows flagged as low-reliability. 3 portcos assigned Grade C. Scope 3 explicitly flagged as screening-grade, not disclosure-grade. ESG scorecard cross-checked against sector peers.
  4. Assumption Flagging. eGRID applied at state level (±15% vs subregion). Scope 3 ±50%. ESG scores are judgment-based pending populated Supabase esg_portco_scores table.
