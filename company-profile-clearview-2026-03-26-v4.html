Status: Installed, but anti-idle policy not yet enforced.

Revenue Risk — Critical Finding
Clearview lost an estimated $2–3M in contract value in 2025 due to poor ESG scores on competitive RFPs. Five RFPs included ESG scoring criteria where Clearview was outscored by competitors with formal climate programs.

As an environmental services company, Clearview faces asymmetric reputational risk — clients expect their environmental consultant to have its own house in order. Completing the emissions baseline and setting reduction targets is not just good practice; it is directly revenue-protective.
