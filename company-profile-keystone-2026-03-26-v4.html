Healthcare-Specific Climate Risk Insight
Climate risk for behavioral health is fundamentally different from other sectors. The primary risk is not facility damage — it's service disruption. When clinics close for weather events, 15-20% of patients experience clinical regression within 2-3 weeks. This translates to: longer treatment courses (increased cost), patient churn (lost revenue), and clinical outcome deterioration (regulatory/quality risk). Telehealth is both a climate adaptation strategy and an emission reduction strategy.
